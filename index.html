About Us

Welcome to our church! We are a community dedicated to faith, worship, and service. Below, you can learn about our core values, mission, and vision.

Our Core Values
  • We are a Christian People. The Holy Spirit transforms believers into individuals marked by deep love, spiritual discipline, moral purity, compassion for others, and a commitment to justice.
  • We are a Holiness People. The Church is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and nurturing disciple-making community, deeply rooted in the Wesleyan-Holiness tradition.
  • We are a Missional People. The Church is deeply committed to engaging in compassionate outreach that addresses the needs of our community and beyond.
Our Mission

“To be a Christ-like disciple and to make Christ-like disciples in Tanza and beyond.”

Our mission is to embody the teachings of Christ by striving to be Christ-like disciples in our community of Tanza and extending this commitment beyond. This mission is the cornerstone of our identity and purpose, defining who we are, why we exist, and the core reason for our being. We are dedicated to living out the example of Jesus Christ through acts of love, humility, and service, and we are equally committed to guiding others in their journey to becoming Christ-like disciples. Our goal is to foster a nurturing environment where individuals are equipped with spiritual tools.

Our Vision
  • Engage our community. Make sure to actively involve and connect with local individuals and groups to build relationship and empowerment.
  • Establish strong spiritual foundations. Create basis for spiritual growth through teaching, worship, support and personal development.
  • Equip believers to serve. Provide resources and training to help individuals effectively use their gifts and talents in service.
  • Empower disciples to go and make disciples. Encourage and support believers in sharing their faith and mentoring others in their spiritual journey.
